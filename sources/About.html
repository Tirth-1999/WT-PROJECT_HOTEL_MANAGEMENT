ONLINE AUDITORIUM MANAGEMENT SYSTEM


INTRODUCTION
The purpose of this Software is to help us project. It is provided with some requirements which are used in Student Submission System. All parts, design, coding and testing will be prepared with helping of SRS.
Product scope The scope of this project is to develop a platform to book and pre-look the auditoriums, halls which shall be used by people.
They can get a proper idea according to their capacity and needs. This website has the following merits or benefits:
• The Platform will have filters for auditoriums, according to which they can select their auditoriums.
• They can even have a virtual 360 degree look of the auditorium, so that they can check about the environment it has.
Product Overview
Our product’s main aim to provide users with a website, a platform where they can find a proper Auditorium.
They can also share their reviews about the halls, facility they were provided with. They can even have a virtual 360 degree look of the auditorium, so that they can check about the environment it has.
